UPilot Project

Project description

Head of Programming Area

UPilot is a project that was born from the initiative of different students to design and create a remote aircaft.

During the project, it was necessary some aeronautical courses, which were taught by Dixeo. Some of the courses were:

  • Introduction to aircaft design

  • Introduction to fixed wing drone design

The principal objective of the project was to participate in two main competitions: SAE Aerodesign and SAE Aerodesign México.

Being the head of the programming area, my activities were the research for the communication between the plane and the remote control, in the same way giving support to the mechanical design area with simulations in SolidWorks.
